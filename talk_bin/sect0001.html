1 Introduction

We try to keep this self-contained, but realistically a small amount of understanding of tensor products, dual spaces and basic category theory is required. Plan:

  • Tensor products →\xrightarrow {}​ monoidal categories. Examples: Vect\textbf{Vect}Vect, Rep(G).

  • Dual vector spaces →\xrightarrow {}​ rigid monoidal categories. Examples: Vectfd\textbf{Vect}^\text {fd}Vectfd, Rep(G)fd^\text {fd}fd.

  • Definition of Hopf algebras (Examples: Group algebras, tensor/symmetric algebras, universal enveloping algebra)

    • HHH-Mod is monoidal and HHH-Modfd^\text {fd}fd is rigid monoidal.

    • HHH-Mod generalises Rep(G)(G)(G)

    • Define quasitriangular Hopf algebras.

  • Define braided monoidal category. Examples: Vect\textbf{Vect}Vect, HHH-Mod for quasitriangular HHH.

  • Application: Knot theory.
